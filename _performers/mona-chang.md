bio: >
  Mona is a family physician in Williamsport, PA who studied saxophone with Professor Donald Sinta while an undergraduate at the University of Michigan. She has played solos with the Hudson Valley Philharmonic and the Repasz Band, where she has been principal chair alto saxophonist for 35 years. She has also played in the Spirit of Swing band and leads the Autumn Winds Sax Quartet on soprano sax.
